Brand · Menu · Poster

Nextdoor Breakfast 隔壁·早餐

A complete identity and menu system for a neighborhood breakfast shop in Taiwan — the toast-doorway logo, a 20-plus-page photographic menu, and seasonal campaign posters.

Made off the clock, with a simple goal: give a small neighborhood shop the kind of design attention big brands take for granted. I built a reusable layout system, art-directed the food photography, and let the food do the talking.

The Family Tee 家族T

Every year I design one T-shirt for my entire family — the same shirt for everyone, grandparents to cousins. Each edition gets its own animal of the year and a concept drawn from what that year meant to us.

Twelve years, twelve animals, twelve stories. I'm digging the older editions out of the family archive — here's the collection so far.
